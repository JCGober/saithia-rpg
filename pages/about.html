About the Game This game is based in the fantasy world of Saithia created by Jared C. Gober. It is a text based adventure game that functions through buttons. Grow your skills, meet your goals and orchestrate the unification of all the tribes of Saithia. Do you have what it takes to become Saithia's first chieftain?


About the Developer My name is Jared C. Gober and I'm a student coder at UNC Charlotte's coding bootcamp. This is my first attempt at an RPG game. I was inspired to make this game based off the lore I wrote for a series of books I'm currently writing. I live on a farm, where I raise cattle and chickens. I also have 2 cats and 2 dogs. Before deciding that I wanted to become a full stack web developer I was a nurse for four years.
